Mabuhay! I am Lloydie from the Philippines.

I am a Mathematical Physicist turned Data Scientist, and currently exploring the interdisplinary field of Earth System Science.

I earned my B.Sc. Physics degree as a DOST RA 7687 Scholar from National Institute of physics at the University of the Philipines, Diliman under the supervision of Prof. Eric Galapon in his QuantMath Subgroup. I continue working with Prof. Galapon through my M. Sc. Physics degree while being employed as instructor at the same institution. His passion towards understanding quantum systems led him to develop his so-called finite-part integration, which is the focus of my research for my B.Sc. and M.Sc. degree. In our published paper, we elaborated the method of finite-part integration and extended its applicability.

At the start of the year, I decided to leave the academe and started working as a data scientist at the Security Bank in the Philippines. Using supervised machine learning, I made a churn model of deposit customers for bank retention program and started a propensity model of credit card customers for the bank cross-selling initiative. But life indeed unpredictable, my passion in science brings me here in Trieste, Italy to finally realized my childhood dreams.

Currently, I am a postgraduate diploma student here at The Abdus Salam International Centre for Theoretical Physics for a year, and I will be taught and mentored by notable scientist of Earth System Physics.
